Monopoly
Bidding War

Monopoly Bidding War is a monopoly version, designed by a team of 5 and programmed by team of 2 as a school project.
We wanted to do something different than drawing a rectangle on a web browser, so we tried to imitate an isometric tilemap design, but since we weren't allowed to use engines, we tried to implement it with maths and perspective. In this monopoly version, we tried to solve problems of the original monopoly while trying to keep the spirit and the basic rules. While you can still trade, go to jail and draw cards buying property, arguably the core mechanic of the game is changed. When a player lands on a square an auction is startd, and the property is available to buy for everyone, the original dice roller has an advantage on the roll. Players can choose to veto, and buy the property without an auction, but they have limited veto rights.
