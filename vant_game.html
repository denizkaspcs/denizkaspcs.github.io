An indie game
with Unity/C#

This is an indie game developed by me, I have done this project with 2 of my friends. I have programmed the game on my own, and they were responsible for marketing and the drawings. We were inspired by Getting Over it with Bennett Foddy.
I have tried to design a simple and intuitive gameplay, with a very non-intuitive movement mechanic, and some non-obvious tricks such as carrying out momentum and bouncing of off surfaces to pass nearly impossible barriers. The game was a very good learning experience, and we are planning on building and publishing more games.
