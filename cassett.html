Cassett
For Cyclists

Cassett is a senior design project, built by a team of 5. I have worked with Tahir Soyaslan with pair programming and built the entire backend, database applications and API integration.
Cassett's backend is hosted on AWS, its stack contains with node.js, mongoDB, dynamoDB -noSQL-, various map and geolocation API's. Cassett allow for users to track their locations on the map, keep statistics about their travelling properties such as speed, elevation gain, altitude and allows users to track their improvements via daily and weekly statistics.
Users can find homes to stay during long trips and have some company, exchanging exciting stories, finding communities, participating in challenges and much more!
